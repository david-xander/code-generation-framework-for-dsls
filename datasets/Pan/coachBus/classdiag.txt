### Domain Model Description in Natural Language

#### Entities and Attributes:
1. **Trip**: Represents a journey.
   - Attributes: 
     - name (String): The name of the trip.
     - origin (String): The starting location of the trip.
     - destination (String): The end location of the trip.
     - type (String): The type of trip (e.g., regular or private).
     - number (Integer): A unique identifier for the trip.

2. **RegularTrip**: A specialized type of trip. Inherits from the Trip class.

3. **PrivateTrip**: A specialized type of trip. Inherits from the Trip class.
   - Attributes:
     - extras (String): Additional services or features for the trip.

4. **Coach**: Represents a vehicle used for trips.
   - Attributes:
     - id (Integer): A unique identifier for the coach.
     - name (String): The name of the coach.
     - model (String): The model of the coach.
     - noOfSeats (Integer): The number of seats available in the coach.

5. **Passenger**: Represents a person traveling on a trip.
   - Attributes:
     - name (String): The name of the passenger.
     - age (Integer): The age of the passenger.
     - idCard (String): An identification card of the passenger.

6. **Employee**: Represents a person employed by the system.
   - Attributes:
     - id (Integer): A unique identifier for the employee.
     - baseSalary (Double): The base salary of the employee.

7. **SecurityGuard**: A specialized type of employee responsible for security. Inherits from the Employee class.
   - Attributes:
     - shift (String): The work shift of the security guard.

8. **Manager**: A specialized type of employee responsible for management. Inherits from the Employee class.
   - Attributes:
     - hasMBA (Boolean): Indicates whether the manager holds an MBA degree.

9. **BookingOffice**: Represents an office where trips can be booked.
   - Attributes:
     - name (String): The name of the office.
     - location (String): The location of the office.
     - officeID (Integer): A unique identifier for the office.

10. **Ticket**: Represents a ticket purchased for a trip.
    - Attributes:
      - number (Integer): A unique identifier for the ticket.
      - price (Double): The price of the ticket.
      - isRoundTrip (Boolean): Indicates if the ticket is for a round trip.

11. **AdultTicket**: A specialized type of ticket for adults. Inherits from the Ticket class.
    - Attributes:
      - isElderlyDiscount (Boolean): Indicates if an elderly discount applies.

12. **ChildTicket**: A specialized type of ticket for children. Inherits from the Ticket class.
    - Attributes:
      - isSchoolTrip (Boolean): Indicates if the ticket is for a school trip.

13. **VendingMachine**: Represents a machine used for dispensing tickets.
    - Attributes:
      - number (Integer): A unique identifier for the vending machine.

#### Relationships:
1. A **Trip** is associated with one or more **Coaches**.
2. A **Trip** is associated with one or more **Passengers**.
3. **RegularTrip** and **PrivateTrip** inherit from the **Trip** class.
4. A **Coach** is associated with zero or more **SecurityGuards**.
5. A **Coach** is associated with zero or more **BookingOffices**.
6. A **Passenger** is associated with zero or more **Tickets**.
7. **SecurityGuard** and **Manager** inherit from the **Employee** class.
8. A **Manager** may manage one **BookingOffice**.
9. A **BookingOffice** is associated with zero or more **VendingMachines**.
10. A **Ticket** is dispensed by one **VendingMachine**.
11. **AdultTicket** and **ChildTicket** inherit from the **Ticket** class.
