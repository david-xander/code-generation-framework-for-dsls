### **Domain Model: Company, Projects, Workers, and Qualifications**

#### **Entities:**

1. **Company**
   - Attributes:
     - name: A string representing the name of the company.
   - Relationships:
     - A company is associated with zero or more projects.
     - A company may employ one or more workers.

2. **Worker**
   - Attributes:
     - nickname: A string representing the worker's nickname.
     - salary: An integer representing the worker's salary.
   - Relationships:
     - A worker can possess one or more qualifications.
     - A worker can participate in zero or more projects as a team member.
     - Workers are employed by at most one company.

3. **Project**
   - Attributes:
     - name: A string representing the project's name.
     - size: An enumeration representing the project's size (small, medium, big).
     - status: An enumeration representing the project's current status (planned, active, finished, suspended).
   - Relationships:
     - A project is associated with one company.
     - A project requires one or more qualifications.
     - A project may have zero or more workers as members.
     - A project can have zero or more predecessor projects and zero or more successor projects, forming a dependency chain.

4. **Qualification**
   - Attributes:
     - description: A string describing the qualification.
   - Relationships:
     - A qualification may be associated with zero or more workers.
     - A qualification may be linked to one or more training sessions.

5. **Training**
   - Relationships:
     - A training session may be associated with one or more qualifications.
     - A training session is considered a specialized type of project.
