We want to build a system to represent how a bank works, especially how it handles its clients, managers, accounts, and cards.

Each bank will have multiple managers, multiple clients and manage multiple accounts.

Every client has a name and a capacity (which could reflect things like financial strength, account limits, or relationship depth with the bank).

Each manager also has a name. A client can be looked after by more than one manager and a manager can handle multiple clients.

Clients can also hold multiple accounts, and an account can be owned by multiple clients — so it supports shared or joint accounts.

In addition, clients can sponsor other clients.

Each account includes a credit limit and an overdraft limit, which tells us how far the account is allowed to go below zero.

Accounts can also have one or more cards linked. Each card has a card number and can be either credit or debit.
