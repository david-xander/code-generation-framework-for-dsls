### Domain Model Description

1. **Classes and Attributes**:
   - **Machine**:
     - Has a unique identifier (id) of type EString.
   - **Conveyor**:
     - Has a unique identifier (id) of type EString.
     - Has a capacity attribute (capacity) of type EInt.
   - **Piece**:
     - Has a unique identifier (id) of type EString.
   - **Raw**:
     - A subtype of Piece.
   - **Processed**:
     - A subtype of Piece.

2. **Relationships**:
   - **Machine to Conveyor**:
     - A Machine can optionally have one or more outgoing conveyors (oc relationship) and zero or one incoming conveyors (ic relationship).
   - **Conveyor to Piece**:
     - A Conveyor can hold zero or more Pieces.
     - A Piece is associated with zero or one Conveyor.
   - **Conveyor to Conveyor**:
     - A Conveyor can point to one or more subsequent conveyors (next relationship) and can optionally point to a preceding conveyor (prev relationship).
   - **Piece to Subtypes**:
     - A Piece can be specialized into Raw or Processed.
