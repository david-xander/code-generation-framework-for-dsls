The domain represents a healthcare management system centered on the creation, maintenance, and secure handling of patient health records. At its core, the **HealthRecord** class serves as a container linking patients to their **MedicalInfo** and **Prescriptions**, ensuring comprehensive documentation of their treatment history. Each health record is owned by a **Patient** and associates with multiple **MedicalInfo** entries and prescriptions, fostering a complete and organized representation of the patient's medical history. The **MedicalInfo** entity captures details about a patient's condition, authored exclusively by doctors, either associated with the patient or external, reinforcing the system’s role in facilitating informed care. Prescriptions, linked to specific medical information, outline drug details, quantities, and prescribing doctors, ensuring traceability and compliance. Stringent operational constraints promote system integrity: only doctors can create medical information; prescriptions require corresponding medical context; and closed records remain accessible solely to their creators. Additionally, safety limits, such as restricting prescription amounts, and the adherence to patient-doctor associations underscore the system's emphasis on ethical and secure healthcare delivery. This framework ensures that all interactions, from record creation to data access, align with medical and legal standards, ultimately empowering providers to deliver precise, patient-focused care.
