The EURental domain models a car rental system designed to ensure operational efficiency and compliance with business rules while serving customers' needs. At its core, the domain revolves around rentals, represented as agreements between customers and the agency, tied to specific cars, rental periods, and associated costs. Cars are grouped into categories through CarGroups, which help streamline inventory management across branches, with each branch functioning as a hub for pickups and drop-offs. Customer eligibility is monitored through attributes like premium status and blacklist records, preventing rentals to high-risk individuals. Temporal constraints, such as rentals not starting on Mondays or the last Friday of the month, ensure operational downtime, while rules like a minimum rental duration of ten hours and non-overlapping rentals for the same customer within a week maintain fairness and resource allocation. The domain also integrates pricing through quotes, influenced by car group, branch, and rental terms, emphasizing tailored customer service. Overall, this structure reflects a tightly integrated system where relationships between entities ensure seamless functionality and adherence to business policies.
