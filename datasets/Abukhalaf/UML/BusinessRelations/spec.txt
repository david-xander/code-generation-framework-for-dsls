The domain revolves around managing complex interactions between businesses, employees, and resources within an organization. At its core, BusinessRelations represent entities that act as Customers or Suppliers, linking them to operations such as projects, orders, and products. Customers initiate projects and orders, while Suppliers provide products. Employees play a central role in executing projects through assignments, which must align with project timelines and budgets. Departments organize employees, ensuring clear hierarchy and responsibility, with rules enforcing unique department identifiers and mandatory departmental assignments for each employee. Orders consist of multiple OrderLines, where quantities default to one and prices align with the product's rate at order placement, ensuring accurate and dynamic financial tracking. Stringent constraints maintain integrity, such as mandatory budgets for customers and projects within specified limits, salary and commission rules for employees, and automated tracking of critical changes like project dates or income. These safeguards ensure compliance, efficiency, and accuracy across operations. The system prioritizes seamless coordination, emphasizing structured relationships and robust data integrity, making it indispensable for organizations striving to balance operational complexity with strategic decision-making.
