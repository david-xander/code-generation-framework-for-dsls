The domain represents an Internet Service Provider (ISP) system, focused on managing customer subscriptions, service usage, and billing processes while ensuring compliance with operational constraints. Customers are central entities, each uniquely identified and linked to a service provider that offers internet plans with specific attributes like price, quality of service (QoS), and free download limits. Each customer interacts with the system through services provided by their assigned provider and maintains surf records that track download rates. Bills are generated based on service usage, with the system ensuring that charges align with predefined conditions; for instance, if a customer’s usage remains within their free download limit, the bill reflects only the service’s price. Unpaid bills exceeding 30 days automatically deactivate the customer’s account, emphasizing the system’s operational rigor. Additionally, customers can only view their own usage data, upholding privacy. Providers manage service enablement, billing, and customer activation or deactivation, ensuring a seamless operational flow. Constraints, such as maintaining a fixed free download limit for specific services (e.g., 1GB), reinforce consistency and fairness across offerings. This domain exemplifies a structured yet adaptable system designed to balance customer satisfaction, service quality, and operational efficiency.
