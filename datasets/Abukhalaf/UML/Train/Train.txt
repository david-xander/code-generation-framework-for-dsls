The domain model consists of two primary classes: Train and Wagon. 

The Train class has the following attributes:
- identifier: A String that uniquely identifies the train.
- numOfWagons: An Integer representing the number of wagons attached to the train.
- numOfEngines: An Integer indicating the number of engines the train has.
- weight: An Integer specifying the total weight of the train.

The Wagon class includes these attributes:
- weight: An Integer indicating the weight of the wagon.
- smoking: A Boolean that denotes whether smoking is allowed in the wagon.
- currentLoad: An Integer representing the current load of the wagon.
- capacity: An Integer specifying the maximum capacity of the wagon.

There is an association between Train and Wagon, where a Train can have multiple Wagons associated with it, indicated by the multiplicity "wagon *". 

Additionally, there is a self-association within the Wagon class, where each Wagon can have a successor and a predecessor, both of which are optional, as indicated by the multiplicity "succ 0..1" and "pred 0..1".
